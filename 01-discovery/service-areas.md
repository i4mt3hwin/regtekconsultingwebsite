# Service Areas — Regtek Consulting

**TL;DR:** This is not a location-based local business. Regtek serves clients remotely, with a vertical specialization (healthcare) rather than a geographic one.

## Analysis

- No geographic targeting detected anywhere in the site copy.
- Testimonial (Dr. Aditi Menon / Menon Regenerative Institute) is from a specific practice but the site doesn't mention its city.
- No "Areas We Serve" page, no location pages, no city-specific CTAs.
- Contact form has no state/region selector.
- Blog posts are industry-vertical (healthcare marketing) not geographic.

## Recommendation for rebuild

**Do NOT build location pages.** The pipeline's default of 5-6 location pages does not apply here. Instead, the site should lean into **vertical specialization** (healthcare marketing) as its "service areas" equivalent:

- Dermatology practices
- Medical spas
- Regenerative medicine
- General medical clinics
- Small healthcare businesses scaling beyond word-of-mouth

These become "who we serve" or "industries we work with" sections — not URL-structured location pages.
