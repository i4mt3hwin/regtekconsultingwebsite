## Market Overview (Healthcare Digital Marketing, 2026)

### Three shifts reshaping the market in 2026

**1. Meta is tightening the screws on healthcare lead gen.**
Meta introduced new healthcare advertising restrictions in January 2025 and is rolling out a **second wave in early 2026** that specifically targets lead-generation campaigns medical practices depend on. **~70% of healthcare ads get rejected for policy violations**, making "agencies who actually know how to navigate compliance" a real differentiator. [Source: ehmresults.com]

**2. Google Ads costs in healthcare are up ~18% YoY.**
Driven by weight-loss drug competition (GLP-1s) and telehealth expansion. Small practices that can't afford sophisticated targeting are getting squeezed. Healthcare benchmark CPL: $56.83 for physicians/surgeons, up from $41 a year ago. [Source: WebFX 2026 benchmarks, BSPKN]

**3. Referral-to-digital transition is the #1 growth story for private practices.**
Practices that grew via word-of-mouth are now hitting ceilings. The agencies winning in 2026 are the ones who can frame themselves around this transition, not just "we run ads" — exactly Regtek's positioning.
